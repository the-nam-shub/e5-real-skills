## What NOT To Do

- **Do not launch a content program as a demand-gen channel for an existing product and call it a media-first strategy.** This is product-first marketing with a content wrapper — the audience is built around the product, not around a topic or trend the brand owns. The media-first model requires building the audience before the product exists, so that distribution is owned from day one. (Source: Jared Fuller, Episode #174)
